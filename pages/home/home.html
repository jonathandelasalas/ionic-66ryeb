What is a Calories Burned Calculator?

Find out how many calories you burn doing different activities. This calculator uses the Metabolic Equivalent of Task (METs) to calculate calories burned, giving a personalized result. Knowing roughly how many calories you expend doing different activities can help you with weight loss or maintenance.
